How can I get a print out of my ticket/itinerary?
Click on 
View/Change Booking
 on our home page and retrieve your reservation by entering your PNR (a six digit code generally sent as an SMS after you make a reservation) and email address or last name. Once it is reservation is retrieved you can take a print out of your ticket.
Note: If you have made the reservation through a travel agency you may have to get your email address updated in order to retrieve the booking online.
How can I print/re-print my Web Check-in boarding pass?
If you have already Web Checked-in, you will need to click on the 
Check-in
 link on our home page and retrieve your reservation using your PNR (a six digit code generally sent as an SMS after you make a reservation), email address or last name. Once your reservation is retrieved you can print or reprint your boarding pass.
How do I book snacks for my flight online and what are the charges?

view/change booking

What is Fast Forward Service?
What Fast Forward?
SkyWings offers Fast Forward Service for a hassle free experience at the airport. This Service helps passengers save time By priority check-in and anytime boarding.
The FFWD service can be purchased through SkyWings’s website (
www.goSkyWings.in
) and Call center until two (2) hours prior to domestic departures and up until four (4) hours prior to international departures and at the airport up until the closure of the check-in process for the relevant flight for which the passenger intends to purchase the FFWD service
How much does it cost?
A nominal fee of INR 650 per person per sector for domestic and INR 850 per person per sector for international is applicable if you wish to choose this Service.
* Pre-book Fast Forward service and get up to 20% off on above rates.
Please note that if the payment is to be made in any other currency than Indian Rupee, the fee(s) will be calculated as per the conversion rate prevalent at the time of payment.

What is SkyWings Early?
SkyWings Early allows domestic passengers who land early at an Airport to get a seat on the earliest SkyWings flight out just for INR 1800.
Terms and conditions:
This service is applicable to all passengers travelling on a domestic flight, wanting to prepone their flight
Passenger must be physically present at the airport as the change can be done only at the airport
Changes can be done only for preponing the flight and not for postponing the flight
The time difference between the desired flight and the booked flight should be maximum 4 hours, this means if the original flight booked is at 1600 hours, then this flight can be preponed to a flight which is departing at or after 1200 hrs. The basis to calculate the difference of 4 hours, has to be between the departure time of the original flight and the desired flight and not on the basis of the passenger reporting time
Request for such change will be confirmed only 60 minutes before the departure of the desired flight subject to the availability of seats and a non-refundable fee of INR 1800 per passenger (fee is subject to change)
Unaccompanied minors cannot ask for change of flight themselves, only their parents/guardian at the origin station can request for this service
This service shall not be extended to passengers booked under group fares
This service is not applicable on international flights, whether in isolation or in connection with a domestic flight
What is hold booking?
Holding bookings assist our passengers in reserving tickets prior to making the payment at the nearest ticketing center or through our website. 
Passengers may call our call center to hold a reservation or may hold the reservation through Mobile application.
The reservations processed from Mobile Application or call centre are placed on hold for 15 minutes from the time of booking.
